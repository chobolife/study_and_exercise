# FactsWise

## Goals
- Goal 1 - Within 4s & 5s
- Goal 2 - With 5s (part 1)
- Goal 3 - Within 10
- Goal 4 - With 10s
- Goal 5 - Under Tens
- Goal 6 - With 5s (part 2)
- Goal 7 - Doubles
- Goal 8 - With 9s
- Goal 9 - With 7s & 8s

## Guideline
- In 1st grade, students need to become fluent in goals 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5.
- Goals 6, 7, 8, 9 are extended goals for exposure to strategies leading to fluency in 2nd grade.
